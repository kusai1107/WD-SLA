Welcome to the World of Computers

Computers are essential devices that have transformed the way we live and work.

Types of Computers

  • Desktop Computers - Ideal for home and office use.
  • Laptops - Portable computers for on-the-go use.
  • Tablets - Touchscreen devices for browsing and media consumption.

Components of a Computer

A typical computer consists of several key components:

  1. Central Processing Unit (CPU) - The brain of the computer.
  2. Random Access Memory (RAM) - Temporary memory for running applications.
  3. Storage - Hard drives or SSDs for storing data.
